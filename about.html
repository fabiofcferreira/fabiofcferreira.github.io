About a geek...

Who am I?

Hey! What's up?


I am Fábio Ferreira and I am a full-stack self-taught programmer, 15 years old and I live in Braga, Portugal.

My interests and goals

First of all, I hate school but love learning.
In my opinion, Portugal's educational system isn't good because:
- All students are being evaluated equally;
- College access depends on subjects that you don't need for your course.
- Classes are too big (90 minute block and then 10-15 minute break).


However, I am very curious about everything, not only programming but also Maths, Physics, Biology and Philosophy.


I love people. Specially, those who wish to change the world.


One of my biggest goals is to build a team of intelligent, curious and loving entrepreneurs and develop new ideas, new products and change the world.

So, when and how did it all start?

I was 3 years old when I used a computer for the first time. Those "rocks" with B&W screens.
But my passion for programming and design were born when my godfather started talking about some friends he had that wrote software. I was kinda curious about how it was done. This was back in 2012 when I was only 11 years old.

I spent some days googling about programming and I "discovered" VB.net. I started watching some tutorials on YouTube (on my free time). And in July of 2014 I bought my first programming book (Visual Basic programming book) which I read in 2 weeks.

In September/October of 2014 I googled about websites to learn programming. I found Codecademy and learned PHP, JavaScript, Python and HTML/CSS. Since then I've been reading free eBooks and practising my programming skills.

In the end...

I just want to change the world with a very good workgroup. My biggest wish is to improve people's lives.
